About Us

The company School Supply Box was founded by two moms that were fed up with the annual hunt for school supplies and wanted to make the process much easier for the moms that came behind us. Because we buy in bulk, we are able to negotiate much better prices than regular stores have. We are also able to eliminate the problem of needing to hunt at different stores, saving moms an enormous amount of time. We stay away from generic brands and buy the brand names that represent the best quality such as Crayola, Ticonderoga, Sharpie, Pilot, and Mead. Our kids loved the boxes we found to put your child's supplies in. We know yours will be excited about opening their personal boxes in the mail. Let our struggles solve the Annual School Supply Hunt for you.
